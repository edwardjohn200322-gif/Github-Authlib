iii. Why is OAuth considered more secure than traditional login?
OAuth is more secure than traditional login because it does not share your password with third-party apps. 
Instead, it uses temporary access tokens with limited permissions, so your actual credentials are never exposed. 
This reduces the risk of password leaks and account compromise.
